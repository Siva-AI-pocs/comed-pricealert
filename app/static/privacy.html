Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

ComEd Price Alert ("the App") is a personal energy management application that displays real-time ComEd electricity pricing and sends price alerts. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and hashed password when you create an account.
  • Alert preferences: Email address, Telegram Chat ID, or WhatsApp number you provide for price alert subscriptions, along with your alert threshold settings.
  • ComEd usage data (optional): If you connect your ComEd account via Green Button Connect, we access your 15-minute electricity interval usage data (up to 13 months of history) using OAuth 2.0 authorization. Your ComEd username and password are never shared with this App.
  • Price data: Real-time and historical ComEd 5-minute interval pricing fetched from ComEd's public API.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To send you price alerts via your chosen notification channels (email, Telegram, WhatsApp).
  • To display your personalized energy usage alongside real-time pricing (if ComEd account is connected).
  • To maintain your account and subscription preferences.
  • We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose beyond operating this App.

4. ComEd Green Button Data

If you choose to connect your ComEd account:

  • We access your 15-minute interval electricity usage data via ComEd's Green Button Connect My Data program.
  • Your ComEd OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-128 (Fernet) encryption.
  • You can revoke access at any time by clicking "Disconnect" in the App, or by removing access directly from your ComEd account settings at secure.comed.com.
  • We retain your usage data only as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Render (Oregon, USA).
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest.

6. Data Retention

  • Price data is retained for up to 7 days of history.
  • Account and subscription data is retained until you delete your account.
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at rdsiva@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to deliver notifications:

  • Gmail SMTP — for email alerts
  • Telegram Bot API — for Telegram alerts
  • Twilio / Meta WhatsApp Business API — for WhatsApp alerts

Your notification contact details (email/phone/chat ID) are transmitted to these services solely to deliver your requested alerts.

8. Your Rights

  • You may view, update, or delete your subscription preferences at any time from the dashboard.
  • You may disconnect your ComEd account at any time.
  • You may request complete deletion of your account and data by emailing rdsiva@gmail.com.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact: rdsiva@gmail.com
